When you run this command Hesa will spam the text you put into the second argument of the command however many times you put in the first argument.
The limit is 30 messages. He sends 5 messages every 5 seconds due to a limitation in Discord.

*Usage:*
`hesa status | <"online", "idle", "dnd", "offline", "msg: [text] / [status_type]">`

*Examples:*
`hesa status | dnd` will set Hesa's status to do not disturb, while playing nothing
`hesa status | msg: Hello / idle` will set Hesa's status to "Hello", while being idle
